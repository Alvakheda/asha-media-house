Asha Media House is proud to announce the launch of its **publishing division** — and with it, the release of our first two books.

Both are works of poetry. Both are deeply personal. And together, they mark the beginning of something we have long believed in: that local voices, given a platform, can reach far beyond the places that shaped them.

---

## The Heart of Chaos — Mercurial Tales by Niharika HG

![The Heart of Chaos by Niharika HG](/The%20Heart%20of%20Chaos.jpeg)

In the middle of noise, movement, and endless thoughts, there exists a quiet space within us. *The Heart of Chaos* is a collection of poems inspired by everyday moments where emotions unfold gently and honestly. It is about feeling everything, losing yourself, and discovering that even chaos has a rhythm — one that can lead you back to peace.

## Vandhana — by Suhan J Bangera

![Vandhana by Suhan J Bangera](/Vandhana.jpeg)

*Vandhana* is a poetry collection built around a single name.

From the trembling wonder of a first encounter to the quiet throne of a man who has learned to carry his own weight, these poems trace what happens when a name becomes more than a person — when it becomes a mirror, a longing, a world.

Tender and unflinching. Intimate and earned.

Some names, once spoken, never quite let go.

## Introducing the Asha Media House Publishing Division

These two books mark the formal launch of **Asha Media House Publishing** — a new vertical dedicated to bringing original literary and creative works to readers.

We believe that publishing, like journalism, is an act of trust. An author trusts us with their work. Readers trust us to bring them something worth their time. We take that responsibility seriously.

Our publishing division will focus on:

- Poetry and literary fiction  
- Regional voices and local stories  
- Works that deserve a wider audience
